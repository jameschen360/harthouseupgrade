Our Chef - Kyle Guerin

Kyle Guerin

Camrose, Alberta

I Began my career at the prestigious Walter Wilcox Dining Room located at Moraine Lake Lodge in Lake Louise, AB. After successfully completing my Diploma in Culinary Management I moved on to apprentice at The Restaurant at Vineland Estates Winery. Vineland holds a 4 Diamond rating earning them top 20 best winery restaurants in the world by Access Magazine and recognized at the James Beard Foundation in New York City and Food Network Canada. I looked to further develop my career by working under Toronto City Line and Food Network Canada Chef Massimo Capra at the Rainbow Room Dining Room located in the Crowne Plaza Hotel in Niagara Falls On.

In 2010 I traveled back to Western Canada to assume the role as Sous Chef at Elk Ridge Resort, Saskatchewan’s only 4 star resort & golf course. After two years I resigned from my role as Sous Chef to move back to my roots in Peterborough to open my own restaurant, Cravings Bakery & Cafe. In 2013, one year after opening Cravings I assumed the role as Corporate Chef and co-owner of Kawartha Entertainment Group Inc owning/operating The Venue (Peterborough’s largest multi-purpose event facility), Dolce Vita Restaurant (Fine dining Italian restaurant), The Lobby Wine Bar (Wine & Tapas restaurant) and The Whisky Club (Private members whisky club & lounge).

In 2014 I travelled to Luxembourg, Europe as part of team Canada to represent our country at the 2014 Culinary World Cup Expo-Gast. I secured a silver medal competing against 53 countries including 3500 chefs from around the world.

During my career I have had the pleasure privately cooking for former Prime Minister of Canada Stephen Harper, former NBA Commissioner David Stern, Pop Rock Band the Jonas Brothers, 90210 Star Jason Priestley, Actor Colin Mochrie, Two time Olympic gold medal winner Catriona Le May Doan and First Canadian Astronaut to travel space Marc Garneau.

I recently moved to Camrose, Alberta in June 2017 with my family and joined the Hart House team fall of this year and we’re very excited to share our menu with you!
